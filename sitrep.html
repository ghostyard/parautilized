New Employee Orientation

REMINDER!

Please study your employee handbook as required by FBC protocol. This set of documents and accompanying glossary is NOT a replacement for your employee handbook. It's very specifically categorized as supplemental material for a reason. - J. Bozer

GLOSSARY:

Altered Item - an ordinary object which has been acted upon by paranatural forces during Altered World Events, resulting in the development of unique and sometimes dangerous properties.

Altered World Event (AWE) - an event in which paranatural forces intrude upon our universe, resulting in reality being altered; often responsible for the existence of Altered Items and Objects of Power.

Board, the a group of unseen paranatural entities which empower the FBC and which exist within the Astral Plane.

Federal Bureau of Control (FBC, the Bureau), the - the U.S. government agency responsible for investigating "paranatural" phenomena - objects, places, or elements which violate the known laws of reality and exist outside of conventional scientific understanding.

Hiss, the a hostile paranatural resonance that invaded the Oldest House on October 29, 2019, resulting in the near-destruction of the Federal Bureau of Control.

Object of Power (OoP) a type of Altered Item connected to the Astral Plane, allowing it to be bound by parautilitarians, who can then wield their abilities.

Oceanview Motel and Casino a Place of Power connecting between various different locations, theorized to be a gateway to alternate dimensions and Thresholds.

Oldest House, the (headquarters, HQ) a Place of Power based in New York, United States, appearing as a featureless skyscraper of the Brutalist architectural style, though the interior is a space-defying, constantly shifting labyrinth connected to various alternate dimensions through spontaneously occurring Thresholds.

parautilitarian an individual capable of wielding paranatural powers, usually by binding to Objects of Power.

Polaris (Hedron) an extradimensional resonance-based entity, essentially a type of sentient frequency, with unknown intent.

Threshold a location acting as a connection between Earth and other dimensions, which comes about as a result of paranatural phenomena.

The Nature of the Oldest House

ORIGIN

Initial origin unknown. First discovered by the Bureau in 1964.

NATURE & BEHAVIOR

The Oldest House is a Place of Power possessing a deep and intrinsic connection to various alternate dimensions, particularly the Astral Plane. As a result, it exists outside of the ordinary laws of spacetime, and experiences constant shifts in its internal topography. The Oldest House is believed to be infinite in scale, with the area controlled by the FBC only representing an infinitesimal fraction of the Oldest House's true extent. From the outside, however, the Oldest House is a Brutalist skyscraper of defined and measurable size, meaning the building is larger on the inside than on the outside.

The Oldest House possesses a property which makes it imperceptible to anyone except those who are specifically looking for it. As a result, it remained undiscovered until the Bureau found it by accident during an AWE investigation in New York City in 1964. The Bureau itself later seemingly acquired this same property, making it difficult for the general populace to find. It is unknown when the Oldest House first appeared or was created, or whether its name has any bearing to its actual age.

There has been significant exploration of the Oldest House's nature. There is no certain answer, as the Foundation of the Oldest House has been deemed off-limits for several decades.

For more details, see FBC1123480 (Yggdrassil) and FBC22349999-3 (Mapping Spatial Shifts—House Moods?).
