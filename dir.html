Employee Records: FADEN, J. - Timeline

REMINDER!

1991 Jesse Faden (P7) is born in Ordinary, Maine.

1992 Dylan Faden (P6) is born in Ordinary, Maine.

2002 Jesse and Dylan Faden discover a slide projector in Ordinary's junkyard and activate it, opening a pathway to other worlds. The slide projector was eventually discovered by several other local children, who came into contact with another paranatural entity who influenced them towards violence. After Jesse and Dylan were interrogated by their parents about the slide projector, Jesse, using one of the slides, inadvertently wished away all of the adults in Ordinary.

Left to their own devices, the local children eventually stole the slide projector back from those who had been affected by the slide. Jesse, with the help of the entity that she would call Polaris, stopped the slide projector and burned most of the slides. The Federal Bureau of Control then arrived to investigate the events in Ordinary, discovered the slide projector, and attempted to apprehend the Faden siblings. They successfully captured Dylan Faden, but Jesse escaped.

2003-2009 Jesse enters the foster system for most of her childhood, bouncing from foster home to foster home. She is occasionally guided by Polaris. In truth, the Federal Bureau of Control keeps tabs on her during this time, seeing her as an alternate candidate for the role of the Bureau's director if their experiment with Dylan Faden failed. roams across the United States, rarely staying in one place.

2009-2019 As an adult, Jesse begins to roam around the United States, trying to avoid the Bureau's agents and attention. During this time, she ends up briefly committed to a psychiatric facility, where she is interviewed. These interviews are retrieved by the FBC for further data collection.

2019 Prompted by Polaris, Jesse arrives at the Oldest House in New York City in order to find her brother. In doing so, she becomes the director of the Federal Bureau of Control and begins to deal with the infestation of the paranatural resonance that she names the Hiss, attempting to remove it from the Oldest House. In her explorations of the House, she decides that neither the Hiss nor the Board can be trusted.

2019-2024 The Oldest House remains in lockdown. Jesse continues to attempt to remove the Hiss, even as the Astral Plane begins to leak into New York City and other locations via the doors in the Oldest House. The Hiss invasion itself has been stalled, but the infestation is ongoing.
